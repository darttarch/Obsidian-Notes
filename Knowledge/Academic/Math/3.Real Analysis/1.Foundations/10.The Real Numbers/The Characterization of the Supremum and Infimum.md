Let $\alpha=\sup{A}$ and $\beta=\inf{A}$.
the we can characterize $\alpha$ and $\beta$ as follows:
$$
\begin{cases} 
\forall \ x\in A, \ x\le \alpha \\ \\

\text{and} \\ \\

\forall \ \varepsilon\gt 0, \ \exists  \ x\in A: \ \alpha-\varepsilon \lt x \le \alpha
\end{cases}

$$

$\text{}$

$$
\begin{cases} 
\forall \ x\in A, \ \beta \le x \\ \\

\text{and} \\ \\

\forall \ \varepsilon\gt 0, \ \exists  \ x\in A: \ \beta \lt x \le \beta +\varepsilon
\end{cases}

$$
